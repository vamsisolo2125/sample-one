 1. How can I get most of PI Academy for my Preparation?

A. Here, you can find 5 types of Test series namely Practice Tests, Previous Year Papers, Pre-scheduled Tests, Mock Tests, and Custom Tests.


  • In practice tests, you can find numerous questions from each subject/chapter or topic of a subject. You can also choose levels of the test like easy, medium, and hard. It helps you to gradually improve your ability to answer questions of all levels.
  • By exploring the papers from previous years, you can know the pattern and level of difficulty of an actual examination.
  • The pre-scheduled tests allow you to take up the tests of particular subjects on a monthly basis. Therefore, you can know where you are standing in that subject.
  • Mock Tests make you aware of your areas of strengths and weaknesses in a particular subject or a particular topic in a subject.
  • Custom Tests are the ones that can be designed by students on their own to enhance their performance in the subject of their choice.
