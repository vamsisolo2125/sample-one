About us

Pi-academy is a unique preparation platform for Engineering and Medical Entrance Exams that helps students to achieve their goals by providing additional care and assistance through highly dedicated features and tools.

Our sole purpose is to provide a clear roadmap for students who want to get a seat in their dream engineering or medical college with a pre-designed test series and in-depth performance analysis of each student.

Why we are unique?

Students can get a detailed analysis of their performance regarding a particular subject or chapter or topic at each stage in his /her journey at Pi-academy. We provide each student with a unique dashboard where they can get their statistics updated from time to time. This allows them to gain a clearer understanding of their strengths and weaknesses.

Therefore, they can prepare accordingly to become well versed in all the subjects. As a result, they can achieve their goal score, pass the entrance exam and get a seat in the college of their interest.

Highlight features of Pi-academy:

  • You can choose the level of questions from easy to tough. It allows you to climb the ladder of excellence to gain the ability to deal with the hardest questions in real-time.
  • The custom test is the first of its kind which allows students to design tests of their choice that include different difficulty levels and subjects.
  • When you skip any questions, those unanswered questions will follow you through a series of tests until you answer them. This approach encourages students to practice more and gain more confidence to attempt harder questions.
