GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

Please read the instructions carefully
  • 1
    Total duration of NEET - All india mock test 2 is 180 Min .
  • 2
    The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You will not be required to end or submit your examination.
  • 3
    The Questions Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    • 0You have not visited the question yet
    • 0You have answered question
    • 0You have not answered this question
    • 0You have NOT answered the question, but have marked the question for review
    • 0You have answered the question, but have marked the question for review
    • 0You are active on the question that needs to answer
  • 4
    Bookmark : You can save a question in your bookmark library for future reference by clicking on the ‘Bookmark’ button.
  • 5
    Report Issue : You can raise a ticket if you encounter any problem while attempting an examination by using the ‘Report Issue’ button which is right below the options.
  • 6
    Leave Test : Due to any reason, if you want to exit from the test in the middle, you can simply click on the ‘Leave Test’ button.
  • 7
    Save and Previous/Save and Next : You can switch between the current and the previous question or the next question by using the options ‘Save and Previous’ and ‘Save and Next’ without deselecting the option you choose.
  • 8
    Mark for Review : You can mark any of the questions for review either to answer later or to check the option you choose before submitting the test.
  • 9
    Clear : To deselect the option you choose, you have to click the ‘Clear’ button.
  • 10
    Submit Test : After answering all the questions, you can submit your answers by clicking on the button, ‘Submit Test’.
    • Please note all questions will appear in your default language. This language can be changed for a particular question later on.
